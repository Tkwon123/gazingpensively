Looking Thoughtful, Feeling Thoughtful

Feeling mindful? Hopeful? Wistful? Gaze pensively with me for a moment as we consider big life questions.
  • What if the air is poisonous, and it just takes 80 years to kill us?
  • What are snails even trying to do?

Big questions need big answers. Let's stare off into the distance and ponder life's mysteries together.
